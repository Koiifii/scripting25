About Me :)

I am a first-year student attending the Multimedia Production Program at Lethbridge Polytechnic with the intention of turning my passion for creativity into a professional career. Ever since I could remember creating things and making art has always brought me so much happiness that not a lot of other things did. Other than being able to express myself in ways you can’t really with words, letting my creativity spiral has helped me ground my self and feel more connected from within. Every year I love taking on new hobbies and growing my abilities. Throughout my life I have taken on a variety of different mediums like painting, drawing, sculpting, felting, woodburning, and drawing digitally on my iPad. I would love to be able to take my skills and turn them into a living where I can create things, that not only I would love and be proud of but also the people I would be working with.

Accomplishments


I think of myself as an incredibly strong and resilient individual. As someone who loved school as a young girl, highschool was harder then I thought. I had a pretty rough upbringing but I am so proud of my younger self for facing the challenges she went through that no young teenage girl should. I was able to power through and graduate, while really struggling with bullying and mental health. I came out stronger and became the person who I am today, which I would consider one of my greatest accomplishments. When I was younger I also loved theater and took part in a handful of community musical productions. In the 6th grade I played one of the lead roles “Gertrude Mcfuzz” in Seussical the Musical that our school put on. I had to sing around 6 solos which I don’t think I could ever do now.
