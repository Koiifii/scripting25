CRESTON

WELCOME TO THE KOOTENAYS

Attractions


There's always something to see or do in Creston. With seasonal festivities and other celebrations to your day to day outings. You can check out the local Community Complex for fitness, skating, swimming, pickle ball and many more activities with camps and programs happening often for your young ones. Be sure to tune in for the Creston Valley Thundercats hockey games when they come to town and play at the local rink.

If you enjoy being in the outdoors but aren’t a fan of long hikes, Creston has many parks and walking trails. Locals love to explore the Creston Valley Wildlife Center, where trails and boardwalks wind through wetlands that are full of different winged friends and fluffy creatures.

Tired and thirsty from all that walking? The local wineries and the Wild North Brewing Company offer tastings with beautiful mountain views and live music. You can also book tours at the local Columbia Brewery and see how Kokanee beer gets made right before your eyes!

In summer, the Creston Valley Farmer’s Market becomes a space for local artisans to set up shop for the community, filled with local food, crafts, and music. If you’re interested in learning more about the history of Creston and what makes this little town so unique you can take the day to visit the local museum.

Seasonal Events

U-Pick Pumpkin Patch

Get into the Halloween spirit by making your way to the local pumpkin patch and hunt for the finest squash of your choice straight from the vine every fall season.

Creston Valley Annual Fall Fair

The Fall Fair is one of Creston’s longest running celebrations. Go celebrate the community’s agricultural heritage with showcasings of locally grown produce, preserves, art, livestock and much more. With tons of activities for all ages, you are sure to leave with a smile on your face.

Annual Santa Parade

Get into the Christmas spirit with the community downtown every holiday season. From hot cocoa, face paint, store front decorations, to Christmas music and bonfires, have a merry old time as you watch local business floats flood the streets ending with Santa and his reindeer.

Christmas Carolling by Creston Fire Rescue

Close to Christmas, the local volunteer fire department drives through the neighborhoods of Creston, giving you a chance to wave goodnight to Santa and Mrs. Clause. With Christmas carols and Christmas lights decked on the firetruck its a nostalgic moment for everyone.

Annual Blossom Festival

Gather downtown and experience the welcoming of springtime as a community. Watch as local and community parade floats cruise down the street. After the parade you can check out the street market or make your way to the Creston Valley Farmers Market and checkout what local goodies they have to offer. With live music and other events, this time is always filled with family fun!

Canada Day

Start your day with a Pancake breakfast, then make your way to the local farmers market, then end your day watching beautiful fireworks that light up the valley.
